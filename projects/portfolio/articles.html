I picked this article for the same reasons as the another one, I have background with the topic. At first, it was stressful because I wanted to make sure my information was correct. Cannabis Farming Book Review (Page 20)

When we were picking out books, this one stuck out to me. I have friends who partake in cannabis usage so I figured the background knowledge I had from them would make me the ideal reviewer for this book. I also believe that reporting is how we reduce stigmas and increase public understandings in topics like this. Overall the book was okay, it was marketed towards farmers rather than home growers which wasn’t something the audience of the magazine would seek out and I made sure to note that in my article.

Ilana Rubin: Certified Nature and Forest Therapy Guide

This was a profile on forest therapy guide, Ilana Rubin. I wanted to participate in a forest bathing session but sadly there wasn’t enough time between when I received the article and when it was to be published, so we settled for a zoom call. Using what I learned from Clarence Williams JOUR320, I observed her background in the call and asked about it. She had this large tapestry depicting ducks in a lake with trees and mountains in the background. It could have easily been something I missed but according to her, it belonged to her mother and was a reminder of home. I thought what she said about it was beautifully sentimental and framed things perfectly for the upcoming interview.
